Our app offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Monthly Membership).

  • Manage Subscription: You can manage your subscriptions and turn off auto-renewal by going to your Account Settings on the App Store after purchase.

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
